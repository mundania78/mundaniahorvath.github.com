Mundania Horvath is an interdisciplinary designer experienced in Art Direction, UI/UX, Front End Development and Illustration.

Mundania is passionate about working with others to develop creative solutions that bridge the worlds of communication, technology and design. Reflecting her love of self-initiated projects, she is the creator of one of Pittsburgh's most visible design blogs, Steeltown Anthem, which she launched in 2009. The site was inspired by her move back to pittsburgh and the vibrancy of the art and design scene in the city.

Mundania is currently available for select projects and collaborations.
