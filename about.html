Mundania Horvath (who goes by Dane for short), is an interdisciplinary designer experienced in art direction, UI/UX, front end development, and illustration. She is the founder of the popular blog Steeltown Anthem, which she launched in 2009 as a way to showcase the creative energy of Pittsburgh's burgeoning arts scene through local design, architecture, craft, furniture, and interiors. Dane's interest in architecture and urban exploration inspired her to launch Pittsburgh Digs, a series of original graphic illustrations of unique Pittsburgh residences.

Dane is passionate about working with others to develop creative solutions that bridge the worlds of communication, technology, and design. She is currently available for select projects and collaborations. You can contact her at hello@mundaniahorvath.com if you want to propose project ideas, or you just want to chat about great design.
