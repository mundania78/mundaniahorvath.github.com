About Us

Double J Publishing produces quarterly magazines which showcases several counties in the Lone Star state. Our aim is to feature festivals, rodeos, attractions, and entertainment. We are committed to producing the highest quality publications, we also embrace new technologies and continue to explore the increasingly growing sector of digital publishing. Adopting a ‘hybrid’ publishing philosophy, we believe that both print and digital can exist in harmony, often enhancing and complementing one another in unexpected ways.

Quality

We capture the pure joy of discovering the pleasures that Texas has to offer—from art and design to shopping and style to food and wine.
