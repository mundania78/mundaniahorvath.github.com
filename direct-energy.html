Direct Energy

Direct Energy is a company located in the city of Pittsburgh that gives customers tools to track and understand their electricity and natural gas use.

I worked with them on redesigning their marketing and sales collateral pieces. This was a huge project with 6 different templates and over 80+ sheets that had to be completed within a 4 month deadline.

  • Branding | Collateral Design
  • directenergy.com

A big part of the project was to create new templates for their Post Sale, Case Study and Sale Sheets. For the new templates I wanted to create a cohesive look while sticking to their branding guidelines. The phases that followed required adding new content, photography and illustrations.
