Illustrations

The concept for the architectural illustrations was born during my time at the local firm Moss Architects. Observing my co-workers hands-on drafting inspired me to connect my passion for various architectural styles and facades with the creation of a series of Pittsburgh House illustrations entitled "PGH / Digs".

PGH / Digs quickly gained attention from several publications including HGTV, Pittsburgh Magazine, Whirl, Uppercase Magazine and Design Crush. After a few month of focusing on houses I decided to widen my scope to include different types of buildings, famous homes designed by architects, as well as creating custom pieces for clients.
