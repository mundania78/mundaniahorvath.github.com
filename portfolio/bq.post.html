BuyerQuest

BuyerQuest is a software company based in Cleveland, Ohio. Their focus is creating eProcurement system solutions for different companies such as Disney, McDonald's, Caterpillar and Kimberly-Clark. During my time on their team, I designed approachable solutions that would make it easy for visitors to find the products they were looking for.

eCommerce: Bundle Product

One of my projects was to redesign their product bundle page template. The user would be able to configure certain products and also shop for related items. The extra features that the client can add are in accordians and will drop down as the user clicks through additions creating a summary on the left side of the column.
