PGH / Digs is an art and architectural documentary project started in 2013 by Pittsburgh graphic designer and illustrator Mundania Horvath, founder of design blog Steeltown Anthem and owner of design studio Reconstructing Ideas. PGH / Digs documents residential homes in the city of Pittsburgh through original graphic illustrations created by Mundania and featured here on PGHdigs.com. Mundania brings her design aesthetic to the homes and communities of Pittsburgh, one house at a time.
